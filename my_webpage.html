Projects 💻

As an aspiring data analyst, most of my projects inlcude collection and analysis of data and building ML models for forecasting or predictions. Below are a few highlights from my portfolio:

Predicting bank failure using ML - Altman Z-Score & Stock Price Volatility

  • Built predictive models using 24 years of data for 26 public U.S. banks (13 failed & 13 non-failed), incorporating stock price volatility, Altman Z-Score trends, and other micro and macroeconomic indicators.
  • Achieved 96% predictive accuracy using XGBoost, outperforming Random Forest and Neural Networks, to build a model to forecast bank failures in advance, enhancing risk assessment and early action.

Predicting cryptocurrency prices using ARIMA

  • Analyzed 900k+ data points of the cryptocurrency market and built a Python statsmodel ARIMA model for short-term price prediction of Bitcoin with an accuracy of 98%.
  • Evaluated and matched price trends for the top 100 cryptocurrencies to build a weighted portfolio, and developed a model to predict its short-term price movements.

Predicting weather and climate change using RNN: The PDE Approach

  • Developed a climate forecasting model using Recurrent Neural Networks and partial differential equations, achieving 91% accuracy in predicting key climate variables, such as temperature, precipitation, etc.
  • Optimized the machine learning algorithms by training the model with 9-month data (1991 - 2020) over 1000 epochs with TensorFlow, and accurately predicting climate conditions for 3 months.
